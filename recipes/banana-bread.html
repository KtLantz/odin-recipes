Banana Bread

Description

One of the best ways to dispose of overripe bananas, banana bread can be consumed in the morning, as an afternoon snack, or anytime, really.

It is one of the essential recipes in the "quick bread" category - a (usually) sweet bread that uses a leavening agent such as baking soda. as this recipe does, rather than yeast.

Ingredients (serves 12)

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ½ cup butter
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 ⅓ cups mashed overripe bananas

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350° F (175° C). Lightly grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  2. Combine flour, baking soda, and salt in a large bowl. Beat brown sugar and butter with an electric mixer in a separate large bowl until smooth. Stir in eggs and mashed bananas until well blended. Stir banana mixture into flour mixture until just combined. Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 60 minutes.
  4. Let bread cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely.
